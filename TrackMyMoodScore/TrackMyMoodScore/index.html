Track My Mood Score

Inspired by Bipolar Awareness Day (#BipolarAndMe) I decided to create a web app to help sufferers of manic depression or bipolar disorder keep tabs on their moods and hopefully manage their condition a little better.

I wish to make it clear from the outset that this web app does not send any mood score details to any remote servers, all information is stored locally in a cookie for the sake of your privacy.

How do you feel today?

Please record how you feel on a scale of 0 to 10. The scale suggested by Bipolar UK is that 0 to 3 represents scales of serious depression, 4 to 6 is a stable mood and 7 to 10 is high or manic episodes
