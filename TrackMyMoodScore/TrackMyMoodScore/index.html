Track My Mood Score

Inspired by Bipolar Awareness Day (#BipolarAndMe) I decided to create a simple web site to help people affected by bipolar disorder (also known as manic depression) keep tabs on their moods and hopefully cope with their condition a little better.

I wish to make it clear from the outset that this web site does not transmit any mood scores or any private details to remote servers. All information is stored locally in a cookie on your computer, phone or tablet for the sake of your privacy.

How do you feel today?

Please record how you feel on a scale of 0 to 10. The scale suggested by Bipolar UK is that 0 to 3 represents scales of serious depression, 4 to 6 is a stable mood and 7 to 10 is high or manic episodes
